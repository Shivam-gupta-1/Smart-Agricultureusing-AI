Welcome to the Smart Agriculture AI System 🌾

This AI-powered dashboard is designed to assist farmers in making data-driven decisions. It integrates advanced machine learning models to automate irrigation, predict weather, and detect crop diseases with high accuracy.

🚀 Key Features:

  • 💧 Irrigation System: Predicts whether a selected crop needs watering based on soil moisture and threshold levels.
  • ☁️ Weather Forecasting: Upload weather data to get temperature predictions and accuracy statistics with visual graphs.
  • 🌿 Crop Disease Detection: Upload an image of a leaf to identify diseases and get appropriate remedies.

🔎 Try the Modules:

💧 Moisture & Irrigation

Check irrigation needs based on real crop moisture thresholds.

☁️ Weather Prediction

Upload CSV weather data and visualize forecast with accuracy.

🌿 Disease Detection

Upload a leaf image and get disease name + treatment advice.

👨‍🎓 About This Project

Developed as a final-year B.Tech prototype, this system demonstrates how AI and agriculture can be integrated for smarter, sustainable farming practices. This dashboard is a simulation and does not require real-time sensors or devices.
